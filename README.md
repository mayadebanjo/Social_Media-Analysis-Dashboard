# Social Media Performance Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 5,600 social media posts across TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, X.com, YouTube, and Facebook to identify content strategies that maximize engagement, reach, and click-through rates across 8 global regions.

## Business Objective
Social media managers and content strategists need data-driven insights to optimize posting schedules, content types, and platform selection. This project answers critical questions about:
- Which platforms and content types drive the most engagement
- Optimal posting times and days for maximum reach
- Regional performance variations
- Effectiveness of organic vs. sponsored content
- Hashtag strategies that increase impressions

## Dataset
- **Source:** Social media performance data for 2024
- **Size:** 5,600 posts
- **Platforms:** TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, X.com, YouTube, Facebook
- **Regions:** USA, Canada, UK, Brazil, India, Australia, Japan, Germany
- **Metrics:** Engagement, Views, Impressions, Clicks, Click-Through Rate, Video Views, Live Stream Views

## Tools & Skills
- **Power BI:** Interactive dashboard design, data visualization, DAX measures
- **Power Query:** Data transformation, feature engineering
- **Data Analysis:** Pattern recognition, comparative analysis, performance metrics

## Key Insights:

### Platform Performance
- **YouTube dominates** with 151M engagement and 1.13bn views
- **Video content** massively outperforms other formats with 3.6bn views
- **Organic content** outperforms sponsored content in both engagement and views

### Timing Optimization
- **Best posting times:** 12pm (noon) and 4pm show peak engagement (~90M)
- **Worst posting time:** 2pm experiences a dramatic drop (~20M)
- **Best day to post:** Wednesday generates highest engagement
- **Avoid:** Tuesday shows the lowest engagement

### Regional Insights
- **USA leads** in total engagement (91M) and video views (514M)
- **Japan** shows highest click-through rate at 0.70%
- Regional CTR varies significantly, suggesting different audience behaviors

### Content Strategy
- **Educational content** drives most engagement (238M), followed by Product Promotion (223M)
- **Top hashtags:** #ProductDemo, #CustomerStory, #TrendingNow, #SuccessStory
- Educational and Customer Story content generate most "High" engagement posts
- Event/Webinar content consistently underperforms

## Recommendations

**For Content Creators:**
- Focus on video content, particularly on YouTube
- Prioritize educational content and customer success stories
- Post on Wednesdays at 12pm or 4pm for maximum engagement
- Use hashtags like #ProductDemo and #CustomerStory

**For Platform Strategy:**
- Invest resources in YouTube and TikTok (top 2 performers)
- Allocate budget to organic content over sponsored
- Target USA and Japan for highest engagement and CTR
